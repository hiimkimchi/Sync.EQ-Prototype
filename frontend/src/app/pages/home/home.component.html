What is Sync.EQ?

Built By Musicians, For Musicians

We are a networking platform made for musicians to connect, collaborate, and develop partnerships!
In a sense, we are the musically gifted LinkedIn.

What sets us apart?

We are the very first, and only social platform targeted towards the music industry. With networking becoming such a massive aspect in collaboring and growing your audience, we seek to solve that discrepency, making it easier for smaller artists to grow their network of connections.

What do we offer?

We offer a wide variety of services

#1 Connecting musicians, artists, & producers via interests

#2 In-app messaging to streamline communication

#3 Music sharing platform to preview tracks you have produced
